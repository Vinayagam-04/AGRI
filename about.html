About Us

Department: AIML (Artificial Intelligence & Machine Learning)
Year: 3rd Year Students
College: K.S. Rangasamy College of Technology

We are a passionate team of students from the AIML department at K.S. Rangasamy College of Technology. Our mission is to revolutionize agriculture through technology. Our latest project, "Smart Agriculture," leverages AI-driven solutions to help farmers enhance productivity, reduce waste, and ensure sustainability.

We take pride in our unity as a team, working together on this innovative project that merges the worlds of agriculture and technology. By combining our knowledge, skills, and passion for technology, we aim to make a positive impact on the future of farming.
